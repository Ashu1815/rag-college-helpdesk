EXAMINATION RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Identification: Students must carry their college ID card and hall ticket to every examination. Entry will be denied without valid identification.

2. Reporting Time: Students must report to the examination hall at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. Students arriving more than 30 minutes late will not be permitted to take the exam.

3. Prohibited Items: Mobile phones, smartwatches, and any electronic devices are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall. Possession of such devices, even if switched off, will be treated as a case of malpractice.

4. Malpractice Policy: Any student found using unfair means, including copying, possessing unauthorized material, or communicating with other students during the exam, will have their answer sheet confiscated and will face disciplinary action, which may include cancellation of the exam or debarment for one academic year.

5. Answer Sheet Submission: Students must submit their answer sheets to the invigilator before leaving the hall. Answer sheets cannot be taken outside the examination hall under any circumstances.

6. Medical Emergency During Exam: If a student falls ill during an examination, the invigilator must be informed immediately. A medical certificate must be submitted within 48 hours to be eligible for a re-test.

7. Extra Answer Sheets: Additional answer sheets will be provided by the invigilator on request. Students must not carry loose sheets of their own into the exam hall.

8. Washroom Breaks: Students may be permitted a washroom break during exams longer than 2 hours, accompanied by an invigilator, and the time taken will not be added to the exam duration.

9. Result Declaration: Semester results are typically declared within 30 days of the last examination date, published on the student portal and official notice board.

10. Supplementary Exams: Students who fail in one or more subjects are eligible to appear for supplementary examinations conducted approximately 6-8 weeks after the regular semester results are declared.
